What is LMS Academy?

LMS Academy is Smart Sourcing USA’s online training platform. It supports two audiences:

  1. Internal employees — company staff who train for free on internal and shared courses.
  2. External learners — public registrants who browse the catalog, pay for courses, and can be tracked through a hiring pipeline.

Trainers and administrators manage courses, monitor progress, handle payments, and review candidates from a secure dashboard.

Key Features

1. Course catalog and access

  • Public catalog for guests and external learners
  • Internal-only courses hidden from public; visible to employees
  • Courses marked Internal, Public, or Both

2. Public paid enrollment

  • Buy Now → secure checkout (Stripe sandbox ready)
  • Automatic enrollment after payment
  • Employees enroll free — no checkout

3. Candidate pipeline (HR)

  • Profile + CV, scores, completion on paid courses/exams
  • Stages: New → In Review → Shortlisted → Hired / Rejected

4. Payments and refunds

  • Refund Tracking with audit log
  • Process Refund via Stripe/PayPal when candidate is Hired

5. Trainer visibility & content protection

  • Student Progress per course (completion, quizzes, assignments)
  • Course player limits casual copying; secure video links for enrolled users
  • Assignment PDFs remain downloadable

User journeys

External learner:

Browse catalog → Register → Buy course (Stripe) → Take course → HR reviews in Candidate Pipeline

Employee:

Log in (company email) → Browse courses → Enroll free → Complete training

HR (hired candidate):

Candidate Pipeline → Mark Hired → Process Refund → Update notes
